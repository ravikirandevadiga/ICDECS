SPS Journal Submission

Guidelines

Authors of an SPS sole or majority owned journal can present their paper to the DSW conference attendees. Since the paper has been accepted and published by IEEE, no further review of the paper is required. However, it is at the discretion of the conference organizers on whether to accept the paper for presentation at the conference.

Eligible papers must have been published by SPS prior to the paper submission deadline (April 12, 2019), and are valid for the next appropriate eligible workshop within a year of initial publication date.
