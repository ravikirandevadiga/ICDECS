Student Travel Support

IEEE DSW 2019 is pleased to announce the availability of several travel grants for undergraduate and graduate students, who are either enrolled in a US institute of higher learning or who are US citizens, to attend the workshop. The travel grants are awarded on a competitive basis and are supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF).

These student travel grants will enable sharing of scientific information and stimulation of research interests for students in the fields of data science, machine learning and signal processing. Each travel grant will be up to $750 (USD) in the form of expense reimbursement after the conference.

The organizing committee especially encourages participation of students who belong to underrepresented groups in STEM (women, students of color, etc.) and whose research career will benefit greatly from attending DSW 2019.

Eligibility conditions for receiving an award

- You either must be enrolled in a US institute of higher learning or you must be a US citizen enrolled in an institute of higher learning anywhere in the world.

- You must show the proof of enrollment and, if not enrolled in a US institute, US citizenship when submitting your application.

How to apply

The application should be submitted electronically using the following form: APPLICATION FORM.

The application package must include the following materials:

  1. A reference letter from an advisor or an instructor at your institution (uploaded using the REFERENCE LETTER FORM). The letter should: support your application, confirm your enrollment status as a student, justify the need for the support, and possibly commit to covering any costs above the awarded amount.
  2. An estimate of the costs (registration, transportation, meals, and lodging) for attending DSW 2019.
  3. A statement detailing as to how attending DSW 2019 will enhance your research career.
  4. Clarification as to who will cover expenses in case they exceed the awarded amount.
  5. Where applicable, DSW 2019 paper number(s) that you coauthored.

What can be claimed for reimbursement?

- Student registration fees at DSW 2019.

- Airfare on US-flag carriers.

- Ground transportation charges.

- Meal and lodging expenses.

Application deadline: May 17, 2019

Acceptance notification: May 22, 2019

Please address any questions related to these travel grants to the Finance Chair, Gonzalo Mateos (gmateosb@ece.rochester.edu).
